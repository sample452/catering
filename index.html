Our Philosophy

Traditional Tamil Nadu Catering & Premium Food Styling

At Aura, we believe that catering is not just about serving food; it's about honoring our heritage and crafting an immersive sensory dining experience. We combine rich Tamil Nadu culinary traditions, custom-blended masalas, and elegant food presentations.

Note: We are professional culinary and catering specialists only. We focus entirely on preparing and serving the perfect feast—we do not handle general event organizing or venue decor. Our master chefs and serving staff collaborate to make your family feast smooth, hygienic, and memorable.

Our Catering Offerings

Kalyana Virundhu (Wedding Feast)

Grand traditional banana leaf (Elai Saapaadu) dining or modern reception buffet layouts. We manage the entire cooking, plating, and serving process with pure culinary expertise.

Pirantha Naal & Family Functions

Delicious birthday (Pirantha Naal) and ear-piercing (Kaadhani Vizha) catering, complete with live hot dosa counters, chat counters, and traditional coffee counters.

Grahapravesam (Housewarming)

Punctual, hygienic, and pure vegetarian breakfast (tiffin) and traditional lunch spreads prepared with fresh ingredients to bless your new home.

Valaikaappu & Custom Feasts

Specialized variety rice selections (Chithra Annam) for baby showers, betrothals, and intimate home family gatherings, prepared to traditional perfection.

Nellore Fish Curry
